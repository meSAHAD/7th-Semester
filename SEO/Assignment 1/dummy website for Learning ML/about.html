About This Roadmap

This website presents a structured roadmap for learning Machine Learning from scratch. It is designed for students and beginners who want a clear, step-by-step path without being overwhelmed by advanced mathematics or complex tools at the beginning.

The roadmap follows an industry-aligned progression, starting with fundamental concepts, then moving through programming, data handling, core algorithms, and finally real-world deployment practices.

Who Is This For?

  • Undergraduate students in Computer Science or related fields
  • Beginners interested in Machine Learning
  • Anyone looking for a clear learning sequence
